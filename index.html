Alexander Grothendieck

This page will be a pointer to all things Grothendieck. One the most inspiring human beings that has ever lived. Not just because he was a mathematical genius but a completely uncompromising person that never went again his moral fiber, even if it caused him a lot of discomfort and ridicule.

See FGA LaTeX transcription for a typeset version of the famous Bourbaki seminaire talks Grothendieck on the Fondements de la Géometrie Algébrique. Please find some additional Bourbaki seminar notes by Grothendieck here.
